How to Play

A mystery basketball player is chosen at random. You have 6 guesses to figure out who it is.

After each guess, you'll see clues:

  • Green — Exact match
  • Yellow — Close (within 2 inches for height)
  • Gray — No match, with ↑/↓ arrows showing direction

Columns:

  • Pos — Position (Guard, Forward, Center)
  • Height — Player's height with directional arrow
  • Years — Years active. Green if they overlapped (teammates). Arrow shows if mystery player is more recent (↑) or older (↓)
  • Stat — A random stat category (Pts/Reb/Ast/Stl) per-game average with directional arrow

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy — Stars: 10+ ppg or 7+ rpg or 5+ apg
  • Medium — Contributors: 6+ ppg or 4+ rpg
  • Hard — Deep cuts: 2+ ppg or 1+ rpg
  • Open — Anyone who saw the court
  • Recent — Last 5 years, 2+ ppg
