# Privacy Policy for Zouzzle

**Last Updated: June 28, 2026**

## Overview

Zouzzle is a sports trivia guessing game. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**Zouzzle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The app operates entirely on your device with no network connections, accounts, or analytics.

## What We Don't Collect

- No personal information
- No location data
- No usage analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or tracking
- No account registration required

## Local Storage

Zouzzle stores game progress locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

Zouzzle does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

Zouzzle does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the changes here with a new "Last Updated" date.
